# Privacy Policy

**Checklify**
Last updated: April 17, 2026

## Overview

Checklify is a checklist management app developed by Hennadii Shymanskyi ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Checklify handles your information.

The short version: **Checklify does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data outside of your device.**

## Data Storage

All data you create in Checklify -- including checklist templates, checklist items, progress, and history -- is stored **exclusively on your device** using Apple's on-device database technology (SwiftData). We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

## Data Collection

Checklify does **not** collect any data. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **No usage analytics** or behavioral data is collected
- **No device identifiers** are collected
- **No location data** is collected
- **No cookies or tracking technologies** are used

## Network Activity

Checklify makes **zero network requests**. The app functions entirely offline and does not communicate with any servers, APIs, or third-party services.

## Third-Party Services

Checklify does **not** integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or cloud services.

## Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with any third parties.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All your data resides on your device. You can delete individual checklists, templates, and history entries within the app at any time. Uninstalling Checklify removes all associated data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

Checklify does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
